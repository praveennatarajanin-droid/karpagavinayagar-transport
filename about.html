About Us

Legacy of Experience. Built on Trust.

Sri Karpaga Vinayagar Transport is not just a transport service — it is a legacy built over years of hands-on experience.

Our founder has been in the lorry transport field from a very young age. Starting early in the industry gave him deep practical knowledge about routes, cargo handling, safety standards, and customer responsibility.

With years of real driving experience across Tamil Nadu highways and rural roads, we understand the importance of timely delivery and secure transportation.

This digital platform was created to bring that real-world experience into a modern system. The app simplifies booking, improves transparency, and connects customers directly with reliable transport service.

We combine traditional experience with modern technology to deliver dependable service every time.

We don’t just move goods. We deliver trust.
