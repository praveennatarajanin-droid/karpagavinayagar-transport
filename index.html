Frequently Asked Questions

Answers to common queries about our transport service.

What is your pricing structure?
Our pricing is transparent: Base fare + Distance (per km) + Applicable Tolls. Waiting or idle time charges may apply if loading/unloading takes excessive time.

Current Offer: Get 10% Off your first booking!
What are the Payment Methods accepted?
We accept Cash, UPI (GPay, PhonePe, Paytm), and Bank Transfers. Generally, we take an advance at the time of loading and the balance before unloading.
Do you offer Free Loading Assistance?
Yes, our experienced driver provides free guidance and light assistance during loading to ensure your goods are securely placed and balanced in the truck.
What is your Cancellation Policy?
You can cancel your booking for free up to 24 hours before the scheduled time. Cancellations made within 24 hours may incur a small cancellation fee to cover partial diesel costs if the truck has already been moving.
What about Insurance & Damage Responsibility?
We have basic carrier liability, but we strongly recommend you take dedicated transit insurance for high-value goods. We guarantee zero damage and strict care from our end, but we cannot be liable for acts of God or improper packing by your team.
